Maera “Manos-Secas”

Snapshot

A tough farmhand with cracked, calloused hands and smoke in her hair. Eyes that don’t waste time.

Role in the story

A grounded voice in the chaos. She pushes the party toward saving people fast over fighting.

What she wants (right now)

  • Get the children and wounded out first
  • Keep the party from wasting time on a “hero fight”
  • Find the safest route away from the main road

What she knows

  • The watchers at the crossroads are scouts, not the main force
  • There’s a back path behind the farm through hedges and low stone walls
  • Someone in the area has been marking routes (she saw strange scratches/paint)

What she says (use at the table)

  • “Don’t chase them. They want you slow.”
  • “Help me move the kids — then you can be brave.”
  • “There’s a way behind the pens. No one uses it.”

If the party helps

  • She becomes an ally and guide for the next beat
  • She can point out a shortcut / safe cover

If the party ignores her

  • She still acts: starts evacuating on her own
  • Later, she can return angry but grateful if they show up in time

Tags

Civilian · Urgent · Practical · Courage under pressure

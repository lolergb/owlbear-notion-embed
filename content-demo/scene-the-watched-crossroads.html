Scene — The Watched Crossroads

What the party sees

A muddy crossroads cut through low fields and stone walls.

A broken cart blocks half the road. Smoke stains the sky to the east.

What feels wrong

No birds. No farm dogs.

Someone is watching from the tree line.

The immediate trigger

A riderless horse stumbles across the road — tack torn, eyes wild.

A heartbeat later: an arrow thunks into the signpost.

The sign (player-facing)

TURN BACK.

First decision (force a choice)

Do they:

  • approach openly and call out,
  • move through cover to flank the tree line,
  • or retreat and scout from distance?

If they hesitate (push forward)

A second arrow hits the cart. A voice, low and cold:

“Last warning.”

GM notes

  • Goal: delay + information, not a fair fight.
  • If they engage, keep it short: a couple of exchanges, then the scouts withdraw.
  • Seed the clock: the longer they stall here, the worse it gets later.
